Mandate

The British Atomic Research Division (B.A.R.D) coordinated nuclear research and site security at Windscale and a surrounding lattice of bunkers and laboratories. The emblem is often rendered as an orange‑white eye set within atomic orbits.

Timeline

  • Foundation & Build‑up: Windscale established; robotics and materials science expand in parallel.
  • Incident: Catastrophic event triggers quarantine; communications degrade; evacuation and lockdown protocols fragment the organisation.
  • Aftermath: Autonomous robots and scattered personnel continue limited operations; multiple bunkers remain powered.

Identity

Motto frequently attributed to the Division: Nova Lux Discutit Umbras Antiquas. The phrase appears on signage and printed matter alongside the stylised eye‑in‑atom insignia.
